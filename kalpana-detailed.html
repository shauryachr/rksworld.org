Professional Overview

Dr. Kalpana Chauhan is a distinguished academic and researcher serving as Assistant Professor (Senior Grade) at Central University of Haryana, Mahendragarh. With a robust background in electrical engineering and medical image processing, she has established herself as a leading expert in cardiac disease diagnosis, medical imaging, and smart grid technologies.

Her previous role as Visiting Scientist at University of Texas at Austin, USA, has provided her with international research exposure and collaborative experience. Dr. Chauhan's work focuses on developing innovative solutions for healthcare technologies, with particular emphasis on medical image processing, cardiac disease diagnosis, and advanced biomedical signal processing.

As a Senior Member of IEEE and editorial board member for prestigious journals, she actively contributes to the academic community through peer review, editorial work, and conference organization. Her research has been recognized with numerous awards for contributions to medical imaging and smart grid technologies.
