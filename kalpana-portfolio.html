About Me

I am an Assistant Professor (Senior Grade) at Central University of Haryana, Mahendragarh, with extensive research experience in medical image processing and electrical engineering. I previously served as a Visiting Scientist at the University of Texas at Austin, USA, contributing to advanced research in biomedical engineering.

My interdisciplinary research bridges medical imaging and power systems, focusing on cardiac disease diagnosis through image processing and smart grid control technologies. I am committed to advancing both healthcare technologies and sustainable energy systems through innovative research and academic excellence.
