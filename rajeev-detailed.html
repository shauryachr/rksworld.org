Professional Overview

Dr. Rajeev Kumar Chauhan is a distinguished academic and researcher serving as Assistant Professor at Dayalbagh Educational Institute, Agra. With a robust background in electrical engineering and power systems, he has established himself as a leading expert in DC microgrids, renewable energy integration, and smart grid technologies.

His previous role as Research Scientist at the University of Manchester, United Kingdom, has provided him with international research exposure and collaborative experience. Dr. Chauhan's work focuses on developing innovative solutions for sustainable energy systems, with particular emphasis on DC microgrid architectures, building automation, and advanced power system control strategies.

As a Senior Member of IEEE and member of IACSIT, Singapore, he actively contributes to the academic community through peer review, editorial work, and conference organization. His research has been recognized with numerous prestigious awards including the POSOCO Power System Award and IEEE UP Section Young Professional Star Award.
