About Me

I am an Assistant Professor at Dayalbagh Educational Institute, Agra, with extensive experience in power systems engineering and renewable energy research. Previously, I served as a Research Scientist at the University of Manchester, United Kingdom, contributing to cutting-edge research in electrical engineering.

My work focuses on developing innovative solutions for sustainable energy systems, with particular emphasis on DC microgrids, building automation, and smart grid technologies. I am committed to advancing the field through both academic research and practical applications.
